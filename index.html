Effortless Task Management in Five Simple Steps

Tasketu is designed for seamless productivity, offering a structured and intuitive approach to task management. In just five simple steps, you can efficiently organize and track your to-dos with ease.

Step 1: Add a Task
Enter your task in the input field to capture your priorities instantly

Step 2: Save It to Your List
Click Add to store your task, ensuring quick access whenever needed.

Step 3: Edit Task Details
Modify descriptions effortlessly with the Edit button to keep your tasks updated

Step 4: Delete Tasks as Needed
Keep your workspace clean by removing completed or unnecessary tasks with a single click.

Step 5: Mark as Complete
Click the check icon to mark a task as finished. It will be neatly crossed out, helping you visually track what’s done and stay focused on what’s next.
